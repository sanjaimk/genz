📖 Our Story

GenZCleaning was born from a simple observation: public spaces were suffering from neglect, while passionate individuals had no organized way to make a difference.

What started as a personal initiative to clean up local parks has evolved into a full-fledged platform connecting thousands of volunteers across India. We're proving that technology can mobilize real-world change, one cleanup at a time.

SANJAI MK

Founder & Vision Lead

"I started GenZCleaning because I believe every community deserves clean, safe public spaces. What began with cleaning up my local park has grown into a movement. Our platform makes it easy for anyone to make a tangible difference in their community."

⚙️ How GenZCleaning Works

1

Spot & Report

Users report waste-affected areas with photos and location details.

2

Community Mobilization

Posts appear in community feed for volunteers to discover and join.

3

Auto-Scheduling

When enough volunteers enroll, cleanup is automatically scheduled.

4

Clean & Make Impact

Volunteers clean the area and contribute to a cleaner community.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is GenZCleaning a free platform? +

Yes, GenZCleaning is completely free for volunteers and community members. We're a mission-driven platform focused on environmental impact.

How is GenZCleaning funded? +

We operate with minimal costs and are exploring partnerships with environmentally-conscious organizations to ensure long-term sustainability.

What areas do you operate in? +

We're active across some cities in India with plans to expand further. Our platform supports cleanups in any public space - parks, beaches, streets, lakes, and community areas.

How do you ensure cleanup safety? +

All cleanups follow strict safety protocols. We require minimum group sizes and ensure hazardous materials are handled only by trained volunteers.

Can I start GenZCleaning in my city? +

We're looking for passionate individuals to lead local initiatives. Contact us through our Email to discuss.
